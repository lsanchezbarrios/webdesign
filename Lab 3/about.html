Top Achievements

Since the beginning of the club, FC Barcelona has been a dominant force in Spain and in Europe. When the spanish top flight league kicked off
in 1929, FC Barcelona were crowned champions for the inaugaral season and since then have won in 26 times. FC Barcelona have also won Spain's
cup competition 'Copa del rey' a total of 30 times, making them the team with the most. In Europe, FC Barcelona have won the UEFA Champions League
five times, placing them in the top ranks. One of FC Barcelona's greatest achievement happened in 2009, when the club won all six major trophies they
could win, making the club the first in soccer history to complete a 'Sextete'.
