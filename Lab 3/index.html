Futbol Club Barcelona

FC Barcelona is a soccer club based in Barcelona, Spain. The club was founded in in 1899 by buisnessman Joan Gamper and
a group of other soccer players. Since the begining the club has attracted thousands of fans and constantly keeps growing. FC Barcelona is
identified by wearing its teams colors, Red and Blue. Currently FC Barcelona plays in spain's top flight league, La Liga and in other major
competitions as well. To this day FC Barcelona remains one of the best soccer clubs in the world.
