My Favorite Sports Car

- The 2020 Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat is my most loved sports car. -

The 2020 Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat is my favorite sports car. From the body to the engine, I love every part of the 2020 Challenger.
The 2020 Challenger SRT Hellcat comes with a 6.2-liter, V-8 Engine that will surely make heads turn. This car has all of the power that you are
looking for, if you love speed.

Both interior and exterior of the 2020 Challenger are a thing of beauty. This car comes with a suprisingly spacious inside, and includes beautiful leather seats.
The exterior is amazing as well, it has an agressive look that will makes it look superior to its competitors. Overall, the 2020 Challenger SRT Hellcat is a very
fascinating car, and I will look to owning one in the future.
